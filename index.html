BORJA CALVO

I recieved my master's degree in Biochemistry from the Univesity of the Basque Country in 1999 and, after two years working, I took a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the same university. In 2004 I earned the bachelor's degree and in 2008 the PhD in Computer Science.

Since 2011 I am lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence of the University of the Basque Country.

Currently, I am supervising several PhD students researching in topics covering the analysis of reproducibility, study of intratumoral heterogeneity or prediction of car chashes.
