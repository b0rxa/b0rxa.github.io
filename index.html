Short Bio

I recieved my master's degree in Biochemistry from the Univesity of the Basque Country in 1999 and, after two years working, I took a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the same university. In 2004 I earned the bachelor's degree and in 2008 the PhD in Computer Science.

Since 2011 I am lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence of the University of the Basque Country. Beyond the teaching commitments, I am the coordinator of the UPV/EHU Masters Degree in Computational Egnineering and Intelligent Systems and Vicedean of International Affairs and Research at the Faculty of Computer Science.

I am also member of Zientziaren Giltzak Elkartea, an association dedicated to scientific dissemination.
